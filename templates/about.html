ABOUT US

Plant Disease Detector is a web-based application crafted to assist farmers, botanists, and agriculture enthusiasts in accurately identifying plant leaf conditions using machine learning.

Our application specifically distinguishes between Early Blight, Late Blight, and Healthy leaf conditions. By simply uploading a photo of a plant leaf, our intelligent model analyzes the image and predicts the type of disease affecting it â€” if any.

This timely diagnosis empowers farmers to take appropriate and swift action to prevent crop loss, enabling better yield and promoting sustainable agricultural practices.

The underlying model is trained using state-of-the-art deep learning techniques to ensure accurate and fast predictions. Whether you are an experienced farmer or just stepping into agriculture, this tool is designed to be intuitive and helpful.
